High End Apartments in Gilroy

Looking for apartments in Gilroy? Monterey Terrace offers high end apartment living in the heart of downtown. Our modern units feature stainless steel appliances, designer flooring, granite counters, and in-unit washer and dryer. Located just four blocks from Caltrain, we offer nearly new construction with secure building access, an elegant lobby, and assigned parking. We're also pet-friendly for cats and small dogs.
